Æter – Dynamic FM Tone Engine

Æter is a Max for Live instrument, excelling at fluid animated FM sounds. From soft and discreet brass-like tones to chaotic and noisy synthetic mayhem. Æter is heavily inspired by the way hardware modular systems like, Eurorack, Serge and Buchla are used in the “West Coast” synthesis style. Æter uses audio rate modulation to shape and animate the tone engine.

Stay tuned for more information and sound examples. Estimated relase date is late sommer 2016.
